What is Strabismus?

Strabismus, also known as crossed eyes, is a condition in which the eyes do not properly align with each other when looking at an object. From Wikipedia

What Causes Strabismus?

Strabismus can be seen in Down syndrome, Loeys-Dietz syndrome, cerebral palsy, and Edwards syndrome. The risk is increased among those with a family history of the condition From Wikipedia
